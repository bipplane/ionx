Disclaimer: The IGP includes offers that were made under the Aptitude-based Admissions (ABA) framework. The grade profiles refer to the grades scored by 'A'-level applicants in their 3 H2 and 1 H1 subjects. For the purpose of this exercise, Grade "C" is assumed for both General Paper (GP) and Project Work (PW) in determining the grade profiles. Please note that certain programmes may require grades higher than "C" for GP.

Increased Competitiveness: With the transition from the 90 RP to the 70 RP system, university admissions may become more competitive. The removal of the 4th H2/H1 content subject and Project Work from the base calculation means students are assessed on fewer subjects. Consequently, the margin for error is smaller, and a higher concentration of students may achieve the maximum 70 RP, potentially driving up the cut-off points for popular courses.
